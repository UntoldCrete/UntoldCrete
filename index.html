Who we are & how it started

Untold Crete was born from a simple idea: to share the island we love through everyday stories, slow walks and genuine encounters. We collaborate with locals who know the rhythms of each neighborhood and the flavors that make Crete unforgettable. Our approach is intimate and flexible — small groups, easy pace, authentic stops. We begin from Heraklion and expand to hand‑picked experiences across the island.
